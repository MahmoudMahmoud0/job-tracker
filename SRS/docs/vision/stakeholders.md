# Stakeholders

## Internal Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Interest | Primary Concerns |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Product Owner | Product-market fit and release scope | User value, prioritization, roadmap |
| Engineering Lead | Technical design and delivery | Maintainability, velocity, quality |
| QA Lead | Verification strategy | Coverage, traceability, regression risk |
| DevOps/Platform | Hosting and operations | Reliability, cost, observability |

## External Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Interest | Primary Concerns |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Job Seeker | Main end user | Ease of use, reminders, accuracy |
| Career Coach | Advisory user | Shared visibility, reporting |
| Recruiter Contact | Indirect participant | Professional follow-up, scheduling |
