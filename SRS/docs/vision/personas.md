# Personas

## Persona 1: Active Job Seeker

- Applies to 10 to 30 roles per week
- Needs fast entry and clear status tracking
- Wants reminders for follow-up and interview preparation

## Persona 2: Organized Career Switcher

- Researches companies deeply before applying
- Values notes, tags, and contact history
- Needs a view across industries, roles, and salary ranges

## Persona 3: Career Coach

- Helps multiple clients stay consistent
- Wants dashboards, accountability checkpoints, and progress snapshots
- Needs structured data rather than disconnected anecdotes
