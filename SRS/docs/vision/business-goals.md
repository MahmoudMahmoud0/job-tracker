# Business Goals

## Primary Goals

1. Reduce the friction of managing a job search from multiple tools.
2. Improve the user's ability to follow through on applications and interviews.
3. Provide measurable insight into pipeline health and search outcomes.
4. Create a foundation for future premium features such as coaching, templates, and collaboration.

## Business Outcomes

- higher retention through daily utility
- better activation through fast setup and simple workflows
- stronger upgrade potential through advanced reports and reminders
- reusable domain model for adjacent career tools

## Product Principles

- speed over ceremony
- clarity over complexity
- actionable data over vanity metrics
- flexible enough for power users without intimidating new users
