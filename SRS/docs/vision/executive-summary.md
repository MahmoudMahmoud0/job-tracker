# Executive Summary

Job Tracker is a productivity platform for job seekers and career coaches who need a reliable way to manage applications, interviews, company research, and follow-ups. The product replaces fragmented spreadsheets and notes with a structured workflow and searchable history.

## Problem Statement

Job seekers often manage their search through a mix of spreadsheets, email, sticky notes, and memory. This produces inconsistent tracking, missed deadlines, and weak visibility into what is working.

## Proposed Solution

Job Tracker centralizes the entire process:

- applications are recorded once and updated through a clear lifecycle
- interviews and follow-ups are tied to the relevant application
- company records keep context in one place
- dashboards turn activity into insight

## Success Indicators

- users can create and update applications in under one minute
- upcoming interviews and overdue follow-ups are always visible
- the system produces meaningful funnel metrics by status, source, and company
- users feel more confident and organized during a job search
